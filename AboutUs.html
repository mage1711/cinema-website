About Us:

History:

The journey started in year 2020 with 5 students aiming to create an effective and attractive cinema reservation website for their internet programming project.

The goal of the website is to provide excellent service for reserving tickets to watch the movies in our cinema using accurate schedules and reliable ticket registration system.
